Hello! I'm Devin Warrick,

a non-traditional Full-Stack Engineer with experience building Front-End and Back-End applications.

Before I found myself in the tech sphere, I was pursuing a career path in music composition, taking classes at the University of North Texas and the Berklee College of Music. After some time, I decided to create a webpage to advertise my work, and it was then that I discovered a passion for web development.

What was once a world of musical composition became a world of component composition, route and API creation, database connection. The drive I had to create and share impactful music transposed itself into a drive to build and deploy countless features for web applications, mobile apps, and even games.

I joined Lambda School, a coding bootcamp for Web Development, Data Science, or iOS Development, in 2019, where I spent a year and a half honing my skills in software engineering and computer science. In addition to these skills, I have led several groups of students as a Team Lead for various development projects, giving them insight on real-world processes and strategies.
